What's a DTG?

The "DTG" or "Date-Time-Group" is a way of representing time which I've carried over from my days as a civilian employee of the Navy. I like its simplicity. You'll have to get used to it as it appears through out my weather map web.

Here's the DTG Format:

YYMMDDHH or YYYYYMMDDHH

where:
YY = last two digits the year in 1900's
YYYY = full year
MM = month number
DD = day number
HH = hour in UTC

Example,

96102700 or 1996102700 =

00 UTC October, 1996

Hopefully, you won't find this too hard to follow and understand.
